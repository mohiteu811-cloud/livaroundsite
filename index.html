▸ The loop

Three roles. One handoff that never drops.

Every stay creates a small chain reaction. Each role only sees what they need — but the platform routes between them automatically.

👤
Guest

Stays in the loop without an app

Get a one-tap link via WhatsApp or SMS at booking. Check in, request directions, report an AC fault — no install, no login, no friction.

  • Personalized stay portal per booking
  • Tap-to-message host (or AI)
  • Self-service check-in & WiFi
🏡
Host

Watches the orbit, intervenes only when needed

AI reads guest messages, dispatches the right staff member, files issues, and notifies you. You approve, edit, or ignore.

  • Calendar synced across all channels
  • AI-suggested replies & dispatch
  • Live dashboard across all properties
🔧
Staff

Sees only what's theirs

Mobile app shows accepted jobs, route, checklist, and pay. Live GPS, photos for completion, instant payout on done.

  • Push notification on dispatch
  • One-tap accept · live navigation
  • Photo checklist · UPI payout
