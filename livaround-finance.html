▸ How we underwrite

Built for STR operators. Not legacy bank checklists.

Traditional lenders treat short-term rental income as "non-standard" and discount it. We treat it as the primary asset — because for many operators, it is.

  • 📊Yield-aware underwriting. We pull occupancy, ADR, and seasonality from your channel manager — no income proof letters.
  • 🌍Multi-jurisdiction servicing. Currency-matched origination in GBP, USD, and AED. Local valuers and legal partners in each market.
  • 📜Regulatory standing. Operating under structured pilot frameworks; FCA authorisation pending in the UK. Disclosed in full at offer stage.
  • 🔒Privacy-first data sharing. Channel manager OAuth — read-only, revocable any time, never sold.
What if my property is currently owner-occupied?
We can still lend, but at a lower LTV (typically capped at 50%) since there's no STR yield to underwrite against. Once you list and trade for 6+ months, we'll uplift the LTV at no fee.
How does the appreciation share work in practice?
If your £500k property is worth £650k when you exit, the £150k uplift is shared 75/25 — you keep £112,500, we receive £37,500. If it's worth £450k or less, no kicker is owed. The kicker is capped at the 25% share of appreciation regardless.
Can I exit early if my plans change?
Yes — after a 6-month early repayment lock (12 months for SAK loans), you can repay at any time without penalty. SAK loans settle the appreciation share at fair-market valuation on exit.
Why won't traditional banks do this?
Most banks model STR income as "self-employed rental" — which they discount by 50–70% or refuse to count entirely. We're built around verifiable channel data, so we can underwrite the actual income at face value with the right structural protections.
Do you lend against properties I'm still buying?
Not yet. We're a refi/equity-release product, not an origination mortgage. If you've owned and operated for 6+ months, you're in scope.
