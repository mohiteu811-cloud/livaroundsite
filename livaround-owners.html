03 · The data problem

Your operating data is trapped in your superhost's account.

"What happens when I switch superhosts? When I want to refinance the property? When my heirs need to value the asset? My data is gone."

LivAround treats every piece of operating data — bookings, revenue, expenses, maintenance — as your property, not your superhost's. Exportable, portable, owner-stamped. And cryptographically auditable, so it stands up to a lender's diligence.

  • Owner-stamped audit log. Every event signed and timestamped.
  • Exportable on demand. CSV, JSON, PDF — for accountants, lenders, or your own records.
  • Portable across hosts. Switch superhosts without losing 5 years of operating history.
